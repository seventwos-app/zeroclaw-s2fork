# Outreach Drafting Task

Draft personalized outreach messages for creators with status "discovered".

## Steps

1. Query the creators database for creators with status "discovered" and no outreach log
2. For each creator (max 10 per run):
   a. Use the browser to visit their TikTok profile
   b. Watch/review 2-3 of their recent videos
   c. Note what makes their content unique
   d. Draft a personalized DM script (under 100 words) that:
      - Opens by complimenting a specific video or content style
      - Introduces the creator program briefly
      - Highlights what they get (exposure, community, potential earnings)
      - Ends with a low-commitment CTA ("Would you be open to hearing more?")
   e. Save the draft to the creator's notes field
   f. Add a log entry: "Outreach draft prepared — [date]"
   g. Update status to "contacted" only after the message is actually sent

## Message Templates (customize per creator)

### DM Template
"Hey [name]! I've been following your content — loved your [specific video/series]. We're building a creator program for [app name] and your [niche] content style would be perfect. Creators get [key perk]. Would you be open to hearing more? No pressure at all!"

### Email Template
Subject: Loved your [niche] content — quick collab idea

"Hi [name],

I came across your TikTok ([specific video reference]) and really enjoyed [specific detail].

We're putting together a creator program for [app name] focused on [niche]. Early creators get [key perks: free access, revenue share, featured placement].

Would you be open to a quick 10-min chat about it? Totally no pressure — just thought your content would be a great fit.

Best,
[name]"
